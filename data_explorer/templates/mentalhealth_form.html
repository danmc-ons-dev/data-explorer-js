Mental Health Indicator

This tool calculates the relationship between temperature and mental health outcomes (e.g. suicides) using a time-stratified case-crossover distributed lag non-linear model (DLNM).

Please upload your daily time series CSV (date, region optional, temperature, and mental health outcome). You can specify additional model parameters (lag days, degrees of freedom, etc.).

Must contain columns for date, temperature, suicides (or relevant mental health outcome), etc.

If omitted, data will be treated as aggregated (no_region).
