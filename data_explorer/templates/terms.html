1. Important notes

1.1 Statistics in development

The Climate-Health Platform has been produced as part of the SOSCHI project. However, given that Climate-Health is an emerging area of research, there may be future modifications required based on user feedback and advancements in the scientific literature.

Statistics produced using the SOSCHI framework and tools are therefore considered statistics in development. See our{{ link.external_link("https://www.ons.gov.uk/methodology/methodologytopicsandstatisticalconcepts/guidetoofficialstatisticsindevelopment#labelling-official-statistics-in-development", "Guide to official statistics in development") }}for more information about what this means.
