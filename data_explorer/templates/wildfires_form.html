Wildfires Indicator

This tool calculates health impacts of wildfire-related PM2.5 using a time-stratified case-crossover approach. You can optionally join NetCDF + shapefile data to your health data if you don't already have wildfire PM2.5 in your CSV.

Must contain date, region (optional), temperature, and a health outcome column.

If 'Yes', the R code will extract daily mean PM2.5 for each region from the NetCDF + shapefile data and merge it with your health CSV.
