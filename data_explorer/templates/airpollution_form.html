Air Pollution Indicator

Estimate short-term health impacts of ambient PM2.5 on all-cause mortality using a distributed-lag GAM with meta-analysis across regions. Attributable burden is computed against one or more reference standards.

Daily time series with date, region, PM2.5, deaths, population, humidity, precipitation, tmax and wind speed columns. Column names below are matched against the CSV header.

Each entry needs a numeric value (μg/m³) and a string name. Add more entries to compare against multiple thresholds.
